About LEDMA

A personal project created by an international student to help other students access useful digital tools at no cost.

Our Story

My name is Manuel García, and I am an international student in Canada studying Financial Services. In January 2025, I became acutely aware of the excessive costs that international students face – from tuition fees and textbooks to subscriptions for academic tools that should be accessible to everyone.

That realization sparked the idea for LEDMA. I decided to create this platform as a personal project to offer useful, accessible, and free digital tools that could help other students like myself. I don't have business partners or external funding – this is truly a solo endeavor driven by the desire to make a difference.

I learned to code from scratch while developing this website. It has been a challenging but rewarding journey, teaching myself web development, design principles, and user experience – all while managing my studies. LEDMA includes tools like a text reader, file converter, and audio editor.

I want to be honest: LEDMA is not the most powerful tool available on the market, but it is a real option for students who want to save money or even generate some income. The goal of LEDMA is to empower students who study far from home, providing them with tools they can trust and rely on.

Every line of code, every design decision, and every feature has been created with fellow international students in mind. I understand the struggles because I live them every day. LEDMA represents my commitment to building something meaningful that can genuinely help our student community.
